Program Overview

The Beginner PPL (Push, Pull, Legs) program is designed for individuals who are new to structured weight training. This program focuses on building a solid foundation of strength, proper form, and training consistency.

With a 3-4 day per week schedule, this program is perfect for those balancing fitness with other life commitments while still making significant progress.

Program Details

Duration

12-week structured program with progressive overload built in.

Training Frequency

3-4 workouts per week, allowing for optimal recovery and adaptation.

What's Included

  • ✓ Complete workout routines for Push, Pull, and Legs days
  • ✓ Form and technique guidance for all exercises
  • ✓ Progressive overload system to ensure continuous growth
  • ✓ Exercise substitutions for equipment limitations
  • ✓ Rest day recommendations and recovery protocols
  • ✓ Nutrition guidelines to support your training

Who Is This Program For?

This program is ideal for:

  • Complete beginners to weight training
  • Those returning to fitness after a long break
  • Individuals looking to build a solid foundation
  • Anyone wanting to learn proper exercise form

Expected Results

By following this program consistently, you can expect:

  • Improved strength and muscle mass
  • Better understanding of proper exercise form
  • Increased confidence in the gym
  • Establishment of healthy training habits
  • Foundation for more advanced training
