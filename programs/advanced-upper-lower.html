Program Overview

The Advanced Upper Lower split is designed for experienced lifters, competitive athletes, and bodybuilders who are ready to push their limits. This program utilizes cutting-edge training protocols, advanced periodization, and high-volume training to maximize results.

With a 6+ day per week schedule, this program is for those who are fully committed to their training and have the recovery capacity to handle intense, frequent training sessions.

Program Details

Duration

20-week elite program with advanced periodization and competition preparation phases.

Training Frequency

6+ workouts per week with strategic rest and recovery protocols.

What's Included

  • ✓ Advanced Upper and Lower body training protocols
  • ✓ Competition preparation and peaking strategies
  • ✓ Advanced training techniques (drop sets, rest-pause, etc.)
  • ✓ Periodization models (linear, undulating, block)
  • ✓ Recovery and regeneration protocols
  • ✓ Advanced nutrition and supplementation strategies
  • ✓ Performance tracking and analytics

Who Is This Program For?

This program is ideal for:

  • Experienced lifters with 2+ years of consistent training
  • Competitive bodybuilders and powerlifters
  • Athletes preparing for competitions
  • Those who have mastered intermediate programs
  • Individuals with excellent recovery capacity

Expected Results

By following this program consistently, you can expect:

  • Maximum muscle mass and strength gains
  • Competition-ready physique development
  • Mastery of advanced training techniques
  • Optimized performance and recovery
  • Peak physical condition
