Program Overview

The Intermediate Upper Lower split is designed for lifters who have mastered the basics and are ready to increase training volume and intensity. This program alternates between upper body and lower body training days, allowing for optimal muscle recovery and growth.

With a 4-5 day per week schedule, this program focuses on building muscle mass and increasing strength through advanced training techniques and periodization.

Program Details

Duration

16-week intensive program with progressive periodization built in.

Training Frequency

5-6 workouts per week, with strategic rest days for optimal recovery.

What's Included

  • ✓ Complete Upper and Lower body workout routines
  • ✓ Advanced training splits and periodization methods
  • ✓ Volume and intensity progression protocols
  • ✓ Exercise variations and progressions
  • ✓ Deload weeks and recovery strategies
  • ✓ Nutrition and supplementation guidelines

Who Is This Program For?

This program is ideal for:

  • Lifters with 6+ months of consistent training experience
  • Those who have plateaued on beginner programs
  • Individuals ready to increase training volume
  • Anyone looking to build significant muscle mass

Expected Results

By following this program consistently, you can expect:

  • Significant increases in muscle mass and strength
  • Improved training efficiency and technique
  • Better understanding of periodization
  • Enhanced recovery and adaptation
  • Foundation for advanced training methods
